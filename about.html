About Vivrtih Journal

Learn more about our mission, scope, and commitment to advancing research

Journal Mission

The Vivrtih Journal of Food Science and Clinical Nutrition is an International open access, peer-reviewed journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research in food science, clinical nutrition, and related fields. Our mission is to provide a platform for undergraduate and early graduate students to share their scholarly work and contribute to the academic discourse in these important fields.

We believe that student voices are essential to the advancement of knowledge in food science and clinical nutrition. By providing a rigorous peer-review process and professional publication platform, we aim to encourage and support the next generation of scholars in nutrition sciences and related disciplines.

Scope and Focus

The Vivrtih Journal welcomes submissions in the following areas:

  • Food Science - Research in food chemistry, microbiology, processing, and safety
  • Clinical Nutrition - Studies on human nutrition, dietary interventions, and nutritional therapy
  • Nutritional Biochemistry - Molecular aspects of nutrition and metabolism
  • Public Health Nutrition - Population-based nutrition research and policy
  • Food Technology - Innovation in food processing and preservation
  • Dietetics - Clinical applications of nutrition science and practice
  • Sports Nutrition - Nutrition for athletic performance and exercise

Publication Standards

Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process. Articles are evaluated by experts in the field for:

  • Originality and significance of contribution
  • Quality of research and methodology
  • Clarity of presentation and argument
  • Appropriate use of sources and citations
  • Relevance to the field of study

Open Access Commitment

The Vivrtih Journal is committed to open access publishing. All articles are freely available online immediately upon publication, ensuring maximum accessibility and impact for student research. There are no publication fees for authors.

Editorial Independence

The journal maintains complete editorial independence. All editorial decisions are made solely on the basis of academic merit and relevance to our scope, without regard to the author's institutional affiliation, nationality, or other non-academic factors.

History and Development

The Vivrtih Journal was founded in 2020 with the recognition that undergraduate and early graduate students produce valuable research in food science and clinical nutrition that deserves a dedicated publication venue. The journal was established through a collaboration between faculty and students at the University of Dallas, with support from the Department of Nutritional Sciences.

The name "Vivrtih" reflects our commitment to vitality, health, and the transformative power of nutrition science in improving human wellbeing. This reflects our dedication to fostering new contributions from emerging scholars in the field of nutrition and food science.

Key Milestones

  • 2020: Journal founded and editorial team established
  • 2020: First call for papers issued
  • 2021: Inaugural issue preparation
  • 2022: Volume 1, Issue 1 published
  • 2022: Expanded submission guidelines and international outreach
